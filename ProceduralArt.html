About:

In this project, I have gained theoretical and practical knowledge about different means of generating procedural content. I have created a system that makes creating iterations faster and easier and thus lowers the overall amount of production time. The product delivered is a procedurally crafted and reimagined small-scope city area produced by using suitable tools and workflows, presented in the game engine Unity.
