The use of generative AI

Currently, I am experimenting with generative AI and researching how it can be used in a responsible way in the creative industry. I condemn the training of models using stolen content without the permission of the creator, and the use of these models. I do acknowledge that generative AI can be a helpful tool and may speed up creation processes if used as inspiration or as a stepping stone to build on. I condemn the stealing of art and designs, and this restricts creativity. Using AI for code is different to me, as anyone can use and write their own code, and almost all possible coding solutions are publicly available on the internet. AI can therefore speed up the process of building software, without taking away the creative aspect, and while still being able to implement the user's own design.

Currently, I am experimenting with building a lifestyle tracker app called WellGuardian to track improvement on Bad Habits and Good Habits, and a daily reflection. I am also building a battle plan app called Victory path to stop watching inappropriate adult content and acting on it.

Congrats! After reading this whole rant about morality and AI, you found a secret game!
