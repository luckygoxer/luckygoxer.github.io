Study goals

July 17, 2025

From September 2025 to January 2026, I am going to do the minor: European Project Semester (EPS) at St. Pölten University of Applied Sciences (FHSTP) in Austria. This is not a specific pre-defined project but rather tailored tasks to the individual profiles of accepted students. From the four focuses: Edutainment, Data, Engineering, and Journalism, I have chosen the focus Edutainment. This focus requires competence in one or more of these fields: audio, video & film production, animation, graphic design, games, gamification, serious games, exergames, and storytelling. This connects greatly with my studies in the Netherlands called Creative Media & Game Technologies. Especially with my field as a Game Designer, because I already have experience in Unity, C#, Level Design, Game Design and Gamification.

Outside the Multidisciplinary Project and accompanying subjects, I would very much like to do the Scouting Austrian Culture which consists of workshop sessions on Austrian culture. For example, collecting and contributing facts and experiences on Austrian geography, history, arts, music, and further topics of interest, including participation in excursions. I love art and history, so this subject intrigues me very much. The Scouting Austrian Culture course was part of why I wanted to come to FHSTP. I love art and history, so this subject intrigues me very much.

The school is located in St. Pölten, which is about an hour away from Vienna. In Vienna, there are a lot of museums, and I would like to visit them in my free time. The Museum of Natural History Vienna for example. There are many beautiful buildings and castles, a rich history, and much to learn. I’ve heard Vienna has a great connection with music, a big passion of mine, especially Mozart of course. On top of this, Austria has an amazing landscape.
