Logo Design

ASVZ Event Acedemy Crew

June, 2024 - July, 2024

In June of 2024, I was asked by the ASVZ Event Acedemy Crew to design a logo for them. The ASVZ Event Academy Crew is a program by ASVZ where volunteers are trained to assist in various events within the organization. Tasks include setup, welcoming guests, catering, parking management, and event teardown. These are some logo's I created.
