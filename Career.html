Intern Game Design at Denda Games

Current internship

I am currently following an internship in Game Design at Denda Games in Hengelo. As a Game Designer at Denda Games, my team and I, consisting of a fellow artist and a game designer, me, will be collaborating on existing projects within the company. We will convert Denda Gamesâ€™ web games to entertainment versions.

Denda Games is a young and direct company that creates game ports from mobile games to web versions and collaborates with studios like Halfbrick Studios, Mindscape Games, and Twin Win Games LLC. They also distribute their own games, like Sudoblock, Kruiswoordpuzzel, and Sudoku, to major platforms like DPG, CNN, AD, and RTL. Together with DPG Media, Denda Games has reached 10 million HTML5 online gamers each month. Besides managing the HTML5 games portal and the content relating to it, Denda Games has also produced and adjusted HTML5 games.

Our main task is creating new HTML5 versions of the classic games Nonogram and Minesweeper in the Defold engine in collaboration with Poki, preparing them for the Poki environment, and distributing them to different online platforms. This includes developing and refining the gameplay concept, writing down the design document for the game, and using the research findings to inform the design for the Poki platform and player experience. These games are targeted for the rapidly growing online Poki platform. Poki.com is a website for playing free online games on your mobile, tablet, or computer.

I will be led by Jacques Neuvel and the team of permanent workers every step along the way. I will use various types of online documentation to learn about the Defold engine, the Poki developers' environment, etc. Based on research, game mechanics can be quickly introduced and tested within the Poki environment. A solid foundation acts as a starting point to create different levels and environments.

Through constant reflection on my work, seeking feedback, and recording my learning process, I will be developing all CMGT competencies effectively throughout my internship.

To enhance my technical knowledge, I plan to work with the Defold engine to create games and integrate them with the Poki platform. This will enable me to learn how the mechanics of the game are implemented, how JSON is used to create puzzles and progress, and how the limitations of the platform are used to create the technical aspects of the games. Additionally, this involves implementing and fine-tuning gameplay mechanics, handling JSON data, comprehending engine pipelines, and closely cooperating with developers and artists to ensure that designs are technically viable and properly implemented.

I will plan my work, think about my progress, and be accountable for my part in the project. This enables me to improve in terms of professionalism, collaboration, and self-direction. By sharing ideas, researching new concepts, and being proactive about my contributions, I hope to make a difference in the projects and the company beyond my original scope of work.
