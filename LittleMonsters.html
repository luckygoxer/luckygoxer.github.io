Little Monsters

July 4, 2025

About:

Little Monsters is a game based on the drawings of a group of primary school children. Our goal was to bring their fantasies to life!

I have been in charge of creating the graybox (a top-down map) and its properties (map size, monster spawn locations, biome objects locations, legenda), a clean, well-designed, intuitive UI/UX design, the UI/UX implementation, content implementation, and content creation. I have created some 3D models in Maya, like the stars, and the icons in the UI, like the starjar. I have playtested with the target audience and written a complete feedback & iterations document, and a justification document. In this project, I could prove good use of professional planning tools & methods, professional version control tools, and teamwork.

The final product has undergone 3 major iterations through the use of A/B testing and multiple playtests. The test results are analyzed statistically, and meaningful conclusions and recommendations are given based on them.

This game was created in seven weeks.
