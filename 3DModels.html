Tree House (With Textures)

April 24, 2024

About:

In the third term of my first year at Creative Media & Game Technologies, I created a 3D Model of a tree house with textures for the 3D Modeling course.
A couple of my focuses were using a consistent style and complex shapes (using beveled edges for example), creating extra textures enhancing the bland 3D Model, and UV unwrapping and welding.
