My name is Lucas van den Heuvel and I am a Game Designer.

Currently I do:

  • Game Design
  • Graphic Design
  • Web Design

And I am developing my skills in:

  • C# (Unity)
  • HTML / CSS
  • 3D Modeling

At the moment I study Creative Media and Game Technologies at Saxion University of Applied Sciences in Enschede. I am extremely interested in creating and designing games.

Ever since I played my first video game; New Super Mario Bros. on the DS, I knew that this was what I wanted to make when I grew up. I never gave up this dream and am still holding on to it.

Making a game is about creating a lively world, special characters and getting color and lighting right. I want to do this myself and especially focus on making stages and levels.

I created this website using HTML and CSS and I will use photos and screenshots to give a good impression of my interests, skills, and talents. I also hope that it gives you an idea of my passion for art and Game Design.
