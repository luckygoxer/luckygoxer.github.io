CrossExamined International

January 15, 2024 - November 10, 2024

As a Web Designer, I have helped bring the websites of CrossExamined Europe and CrossExamined Dutch come to life, by creating styles and lay-outs, fixing bugs, checking grammar and spelling, etcetera.
