Swing-it! Shroomies - prototype

May 16, 2024

About:

Swing-it! Shroomies is a physics-based game, made for Project Final Approach, about two mushroom siblings going on an adventure! I mainly focused on gameplay, UI, and sound. Unfortunately, in the end, we found that the short time frame was too small for the scope of our very technical vision, so it remains a prototype.

This game was made in two weeks.
