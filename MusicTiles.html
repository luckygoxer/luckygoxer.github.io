About:

On 27 September 2025, my sixth game was released. It is an arcade-style game with an unlimited mode. This was the first game where I created everything myself; code, music, sprites, etc.
In this game, you have to type the right letters to make them disappear. For this prototype, I focused on expanding my C# knowledge over the visuals.
