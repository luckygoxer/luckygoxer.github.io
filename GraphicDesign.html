Logo Design

ASVZ Event Crew

June, 2024 - July, 2024

In June of 2024, I was asked by the ASVZ Event Crew to design a logo for them. The ASVZ Event Crew is a program by ASVZ where volunteers and people with mental disabilities are trained to assist in various events within the organization. Tasks include setup, welcoming guests, catering, parking management, and event teardown. These are some logo's I created.

After getting feedback about preferences I created some revisions. The biggest difference is that they wanted a traffic controller in the logo.
After another round of feedback I created a final logo which they liked very much. It is a bit simpler than my initial logo but still conveys the personality of the crew and colors of ASVZ.



During this project, I further developed my skills in Procreate, Graphic Design, Communication, and Gathering and Applying Feedback.
