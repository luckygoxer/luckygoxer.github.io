Edelweiss Valley

Current Project

About:

Edelweiss Valley is a VR game about preserving nature for people in occupational therapy for Meta Quest 2 and 3. The game is intended to be monitored by a therapist on the PC. This game is part of a large project involving five other institutions and our contribution to the GreenTouch research initiative.

For this project, I am pursuing courses on Scrum, Serious Gaming & Gamification, Teambuilding & Project coaching, and Virtual Reality Workshops. The projected release date is January 13, 2026.

This project is the main focus of my minor European Project Semester Gamification.
