July 5, 2024

About:

In the last term of my first year at Creative Media & Game Technologies, I created a game called Chicken Trekker for the Unity Game Scripting module. This is a physics-based game where I mainly focused on learning more C#.
In this game, you seem to have lost your chickens! Go on an epic quest to track them down! At one point you even have to evade the police!
